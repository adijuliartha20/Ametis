LUXURY BENEFIT

To make your stay as much as comfort and relaxed, your stay includes Luxury Benefits as:

  • Return airport transfer with Ametis exclusive private car

    Your personal butler will greet you at the airport exit gate by holding up a board with your name on it.

  • Personal butler service

    Our unique brand of hospitality starts before you even arrive in Bali with your personal butler sending you a courtesy email and photo as a special introduction. He will then be there waiting for you at the airport to welcome and escort you to Ametis Villa. This exceptional level of service will continue throughout your entire duration of stay to ensure an exceptional holiday experience. Once you have check-out, your personal butler will also send you a thank you letter wishing you farewell and a safe journey. This is often the beginning of a lasting friendship

  • Personalised villa check-in and check-out

    There’s no queuing as you are escorted straight to your private villa upon arrival to complete the check-in process in comfort. This also applies to when you are ready to check-out. Please advise your personal butler if you require a packing or un-packing luggage service.

  • 24 hours drop and pick-up service within the Canggu and Seminyak areas

    Please advise your butler of the time that you plan to go out at least 30 minutes prior to departure. A return pick-up service can also be arranged in advance or simply call us when you require transportation back to the villa. Please be advised that the journey is approximately 30 minutes from Ametis to Seminyak.

    * Sometime heavy traffic occurs in the Canggu and Seminyak areas. We recommend that you give us at least 2 hours notice for a pick-up in the Seminyak area to avoid being inconvenienced. Please contact your butler for more details.

  • 30 minute relaxing massage

    Enjoy our complimentary 30 minutes relaxing massage during your stay (one time massage for every guest). Please check the availability with your butler.

  • Welcome treats

    Please choose your preferred cocktail or mocktail upon your arrival. A chilled towel will refresh you after a long flight.

  • Daily breakfast with a variety of menus to choose from

    We offer you variety of breakfast menus, which will be served in the comfort of your villa or at Eternal restaurant. Should you have an early morning departure, we will prepare a breakfast box to take with you.

  • Afternoon tea and sunset cocktail

    Complimentary afternoon tea and a sunset cocktail are available every day. Unwind with a relaxing chamomile tea or our refreshing Ametis Cocktail in the privacy of your villa or at Ametis Lobby Lounge.

    Afternoon tea: 15:00 to 18:00 hours
    Sunset cocktail: 18:00 to 20:00 hours

  • Complimentary Mini Bar

    Soft drinks and beer in your mini bar are re-stocked daily and complimentary. Please feel at home.

  • Home-made snacks

    Please enjoy our complimentary home-made cookies, as well as dried fruits and nuts, which are available in your bedroom. These treats are also available as souvenirs to take home. Please contact your butler to place an order.

  • Coffee and tea

    Our Ametis original blend of Indonesian coffee and a variety of teas are available in your villa kitchen. Please enjoy with our compliments.

  • Daily fresh fruit and flower arrangement in villa

    The island of Bali offers many different varieties of tropical fruit. Please advise your preferred fruits prior to your arrival, so that your personal butler can arrange a selection for your villa.

  • Daily Newspaper (based on your language preference)

    Selected newspapers are available; please contact your butler for more details

  • WiFi internet access, 24 hours daily

    Free WiFi is available all around Ametis Villa. Please contact your butler for the login name and password.　Complimentary one hour usage a laptop is also available upon request. Please check with your butler for availability.

  • Complimentary use of mountain bike

    Explore Canggu by mountain bike. Please check the availability with your butler.

* Book direct with us and be entitled to full luxury benefits.
* Bed& Breakfast offer does not include some of the above benefits.
* If you reserve through a travel agent or online agent, please enquire with us whether your stay includes the above benefits
