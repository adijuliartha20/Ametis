Bali By Candlelight

This is a once in a lifetime romantic journey where we offer you an unforgettable dining experience in the privacy of your own villa. An elegant table setting with a fresh flower arrangement and candles flickering around the edge of the pool will set the mood for an evening of intimacy. A four-course dinner of culinary perfection is served as you wine and dine the night away. Should you wish to have a menu tailored to your own tastes, your personal butler will arrange for you to meet our chef one day prior to your dinner.

In-Villa BBQ

When hosting a small private party or special gathering, a BBQ at Ametis Villa is the perfect choice for an evening of good food and social interaction. Our chef will cook the BBQ in the comfort of your own villa and serve you personally.

Precious Dine on the Beach

Savour a flavoursome dinner served on the beach at an elegant table setting surrounded by flickering candles and flowers. Start with cocktails and canapés as you gaze out towards the Indian Ocean just as the sun begins to set dramatically over the distant horizon. A four-course dinner will be served by your personal butler during this evening of indulgence in the serene ambience of Canggu Beach. This is a simply precious dining experience to treasure forever.

Champagne on the Beach

Indulge in one of life’s most luxurious pleasures by drinking champagne on the beach. A romantic white canopy and relaxing lounger for two will be set up for you to sit back and enjoy a chilled bottle of Veuve Clicquot or Dom Perignon paired with a selection of canapés. You will be served by your personal butler as the sun begins to set over the distant horizon in what promises to be a Bali experience to treasure always.

Beach Picnic Lunch

Ametis Villa is just a 5 minute walk to Canggu Beach. This is a great spot to relax and enjoy a gourmet picnic whilst sitting back on a sun-lounger beneath a shady umbrella. Your personal butler will deliver your lunch in a traditional basket as you take pleasure in a day of sun, sea and surf. Complimentary transportation is available to and from the beach facility upon request.
