Children Policy:

Kindly please be advised that accommodation at Ametis Villa is suitable only for guest aged 12 years and over. Imperial Villa: Maximum one sofa bed is available at US$75++ per person per night includes daily breakfast and daily afternoon tea

Grand Vill : Maximum two sofa beds are available at US$75++ per person per night includes daily breakfast and daily afternoon tea

Eternal home-style dining and Ametis Lounge welcome all ages as well as the children under 12 years old accompanied by parents or guardian.

For reservation guaranteed, the Ametis Villa reserves the right to levy the following charges, for cancellations made and no show:

Full nights charges per villa will be forfeited for any cancellation made 07 days for low season, 14 days in high season, 21 days in Peak Season before arrival date
